How to Tie a Fly onto Your Tippet


Required Materials

  • Fly rod and reel with suitable line
  • Fly
  • Tippet that coordinates with the caliber of fishing in mind
  • Optional: pliers
  • Good eye sight and steady hands!

Time to tie!

  1. Slide the end of the tippet (thinner line attached at the end of the leader) through the eyelet hole at the head of the fly.
  2. Wrap the short end of the line around the tippet 4-5 times
  3. Bring the shorter, wrapped end of the line back down to the hole created by the wrapping, in front of the eyelet and push the end of the line through
  4. Pull the end of the line tight. The wrapped area on the line above should compact down and create a tight bundle above the eyelet of the fly

*Remember: There are many ways to tie on a fly, but this method seems to do the trick
